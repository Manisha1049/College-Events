About CBIT....

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is an engineering college located in Gandipet, near Financial District, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The college was affiliated to Osmania University from 1979 to 2012 and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. The institute received an autonomous status in 2013. Since its inception, CBIT has been regarded as one of the most distinguished engineering colleges in the state of Telangana
